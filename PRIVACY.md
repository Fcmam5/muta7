# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 22, 2026  
**Effective Date:** March 22, 2026  

## Introduction

Muta7 is a privacy-first Chrome extension that simulates accessibility impairments for educational and testing purposes. This privacy policy explains how we handle (or rather, don't handle) your data.

## Data Collection

### We Do Not Collect

**Muta7 does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

- **No Analytics:** We do not use any analytics or tracking tools
- **No User Data:** We do not know who you are, what websites you visit, or how you use the extension
- **No Server Communication:** The extension operates entirely offline without any backend servers
- **No Data Sharing:** We have no data to share with third parties

### What We Do Store

The extension stores configuration data locally in your browser:

- **Extension Settings:** Your preferred simulation settings (blur intensity, color filters, motor blocking modes)
- **Website Scope:** List of websites where you've chosen to enable simulations
- **UI State:** Popup interface preferences

**Important:** This data is stored **only** on your device using Chrome's local storage API. It never leaves your browser.

## How the Extension Works

### Local Processing

All functionality happens locally in your browser:

- Visual filters are applied using CSS filters on the current page
- Motor blocking intercepts browser events locally
- State management happens within the extension's service worker
- No network requests are made for core functionality

### Chrome Web Store Metrics

The only data collection that might occur is:

- **Chrome Web Store Analytics:** Google may provide basic installation and usage metrics (this is outside our control)
- **Extension Updates:** Chrome checks for updates to the extension (standard browser behavior)

## Data Security

Since we don't collect or store any data on our servers:

- There are no data breaches to worry about
- No personal information to protect
- No cookies or tracking mechanisms
- No cross-site tracking

## Third-Party Services

Muta7 does not use any third-party services for:

- Analytics
- Advertising  
- Data processing
- User tracking

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect your data:

- **Right to Access:** You have full access to your local settings through the extension
- **Right to Deletion:** Simply uninstall the extension to remove all local data
- **Right to Portability:** Your settings are stored locally and can be exported/imported (feature may be added later)
- **Right to Object:** There's nothing to object to since we don't process your data

## Changes to This Policy

If we ever add features that require data collection (which we don't plan to):

- We will update this privacy policy
- We will clearly announce the changes
- We will obtain your consent before collecting any new data
- We will maintain the privacy-first approach
